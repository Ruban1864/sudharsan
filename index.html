Our Journey Since 2010

Sudharsan Catering began its journey in 2010 as a small, humble tiffin center run by my father. Every idli and dosa served back then was made with the same care and authentic spices we used at home.

As word spread about our uncompromising hygiene and 'home-cooked' taste, we evolved. We moved from serving neighbors to catering grand life events.

Today, we specialize in pure vegetarian catering for Weddings, Baby Showers (Valaikappu), and Temple Festivals. Our mission remains simple: to serve food that is as pure as your celebrations.
